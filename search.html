About Us

We are headquartered in Singapore and regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore. We are a global firm with six offices across the USA (New York), India (Mumbai, New Delhi and Bengaluru), Mauritius, and UAE (Dubai). We are one of the most stable and long-lasting partnerships in the region (50 PE professionals). We also have a strong ecosystem of senior directors and operating partners.

We are leaders in responsible investing and are aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals. With an exclusive ESG team that reports to the Executive Chairman, we take ESG very seriously.

We are amongst the few private equity firms to have invested in India and South-East Asia through market cycles.
