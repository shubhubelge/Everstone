Everstone Foundation supported Sharanyam, home for
girls in Mumbai to educate and provide for job support and
give the ability for equal opportunities to young girls from

SJS Enterprises Limited, an Everstone investee company supported Kumarappa
Institute of Gram Swaraj (“KIGS”), Jaipur, Rajasthan.

Expanded reach, increased the number of schools, recruited additional teachers, and upgraded
school infrastructure: to educate and transform the lives of the children of the migrant worker.

Skill Development

Indostar Capital, Everstone investee
companies supported Pollinate Group,
Bengaluru, Karnataka.

Indostar’s contribution trained 60 disadvantaged women to transform into the next generation of female entrepreneurs (India continues to be a deeply patriarchal society).

Environment

Several Everstone’s portfolio companies contributed to Siruthuli, an NGO based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu

The contribution helped restore the natural balance through the afforestation of barren land by converting them into small forests, helping offset the ill effects of rapid urbanization.

Social Welfare

Several Everstone investee companies supported
Habitat for Humanity India

The contribution constructed seven homes (with
accompanying facilities for clean water,
sanitation and proper hygiene) for people in slum areas and
underserved communities.
